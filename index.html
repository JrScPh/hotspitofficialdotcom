We are Hot Spit

A queer post-hardcore band from Austin, Texas blending diverse inspirations into sonic experiences. We started rocking out together at the beginning of 2025. As the year came to a close we officially founded Hot Spit. Our music is meant to move people physically and philosophically. Come out and see us so you can ponder the lyrics in the pit.
